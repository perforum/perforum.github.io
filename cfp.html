In addition to the invited talks, the workshop will feature a peer-reviewed poster session. We invite abstract submissions to the poster session that adress any questions pertaining to performative expression of meaning in language and beyond (see the About section for sample relevant questions). Poster presenters will be expected to give a 3-minute lightning talk before the poster session. The abstracts should be properly anonymized (including the pdf metadata) and adhere to the following formatting requirements:

  • at most 2 pages, including data, figures, tables, etc. + 1 additional page for references only
  • A4 format and 2.5cm margins, or Letter format and 1in margins
  • 12pt font

Please send your abstracts to perforum.workshop@gmail.com. Please provide the author(s) name(s), affiliation(s), and email address(es) in the main body of the email.

Submission deadline: February 15, 2022

Notification of acceptance: March 10, 2022

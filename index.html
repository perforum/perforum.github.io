Overview

PerForum is a workshop on performative expression of meaning, i.e., expression of meaning that is intrinsically linked to the expressive act itself. For instance, we can performatively express strong emotions by swearing, and it is the act of swearing itself that satisifies our expressive goals. This workshop is aimed to bring together researchers from various disciplines interested in performativity in human language and beyond. Read more about performativity and the concept of this workshop on the About page.

The workshop will take place on June 3, 2022 and will be hosted at the University of Oslo. The main organizer is Masha Esipova. For questions or comments, please contact us at perforum.workshop@gmail.com.
